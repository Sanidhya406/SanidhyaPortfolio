Projects

Structred Innovation Game

My team of four members and I achieved success in our structured innovation course by winning one of the best projects in 2023. The assignment involved creating a unique and unprecedented board game. Together, we developed 'Dare Dash,' a thrilling race-to-finish board game filled with twists, truths, dares, disadvantages, and advantages. My role in the project included conceptualizing the basic idea, mapping the game, and contributing to the game's architecture and designing, making it easily portable as the game itself serves as the container. Additionally, I played a key role in crafting the content for the game cards and manufacturing them.

Burglar Alarm

Me and my team of four members made a Burglar Alarm using various electronic components, The aim of a burglar alarm project is to design and implement a security system that detects unauthorized entry or intrusion into a protected area and alerts the property owner or relevant authorities.
